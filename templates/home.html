Frequent Answered Questions

How can I search for a player's ID?

To search for a player's ID, use the search bar at the Skills page. Type the summoner name and Riot's ID and press "Enter" or click the search button. The associated statistics for that player will load automatically.

How can I view detailed statistics for a player?

After searching for a player, a summary of their main stats will be displayed. To view more detailed information, click on the "View Full Statistics" button or explore specific sections like "Match History" or "Ranked Stats."

Is it possible to update my stats in real-time?

Yes, you can update your stats. Simply refresh the page or search for your summoner again. This will sync the data with the League of Legends servers to display the most recent information.

Are my data safe and private?

Yes, your data is completely secure. We only access public information provided by Riot Games, and we do not share your personal information with third parties under any circumstances.
